Illenium - Beautiful Creatures (feat MAX)

Illenium is one of my favourite artists. His track “Beautiful Ceatures” in collaboration with MAX is always on top of my playlist. However I am more of a melody guy and less of a “thumpy” guy. Illenum no-doubt has great melodies but the thumpy part bothers me.

Last weekend I recorded the base melody to the music on my Piano, Yamaha - P45 (she is a beauty, and she’s my baby). I played around with the music to get the right notes but it was pretty straight forward. I just kept playing till I could feel the music calm me down and excite me at the same time. Once I had the melody down. I exported the Midi keys to my laptop, where on Ableton I merged the layers together to get to this acoustic version.

But Somehow, it felt a bit blank. So I mixed it by layering (nay, BOMBARDING), the midi keys, and playing with the instrument orchestra that I got going on Ableton. After some trial and error, I cam to this soulful verison.

All that was left after this was to create some sexy visuazlizations that Trap Natiion does, but yeah, keep it mellow. And finally, here it is for the soulful version and here for the acoustic version.
